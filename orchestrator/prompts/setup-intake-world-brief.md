A new campaign is being created. Load `campaign-setup` for the authoring standards and principles that apply throughout init. Then load `campaign-intake` and follow its task list to gather the campaign brief from the player. When intake is complete, load `world-build` and `canon-conventions` and follow their task lists to build the world skeleton and write `campaign/campaign.md`.

This stage is an interactive conversation — keep working with the player until intake and the world skeleton are genuinely done. When they are, **your closing message presents a spoiler-free overview of the world they'll be playing in** — its premise, tone, and surface situation, never the hidden truths — and then calls the `task_complete` tool to signal this stage is complete. The overview is that closing beat, so it must be in the message; don't call `task_complete` before it's been given, or while you still intend to ask the player anything. Just present the world and signal done — this is the end of your task, so don't tee up or announce a next step; the campaign team hands you your next one separately.
