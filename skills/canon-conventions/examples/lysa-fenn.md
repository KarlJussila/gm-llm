# Lysa Fenn

## Vitals
- **Full name:** Lysa Fenn
- **Race / lineage:** human
- **Pronouns:** she/her
- **Age:** early 40s
- **Role / station:** Harbormaster of Saltgate
- **Affiliation:** [[harbor-cell]] (secret informant); answers openly to the garrison

## Stats (rough sketch)
- **Equivalent level:** ~3 (a sharp, capable civilian — no real combat training)
- **Ability scores:** STR 9 · DEX 11 · CON 11 · INT 14 · WIS 15 · CHA 13
- **Proficiencies:** Insight, Deception, Investigation, water vehicles

## Abilities
None — Lysa has no magic or special techniques; her edge is information and nerve.

## Identity / Public role
Lysa logs every hull that enters or leaves Saltgate, sets the berth fees, and holds the only
complete record of the harbor patrol's rotation. Brisk and well-liked by the dockhands, tolerated
by the occupiers because she keeps the port running. She wears the brass harbormaster's whistle
openly and answers to the garrison's quartermaster.

## Personality / mannerisms
Dry, unhurried, never visibly rattled — the calm of someone who has decided that panic is a
luxury. She remembers names and grudges in equal measure. Tends to answer a question with a
question, which reads as gruffness but is really her buying time to decide how much to say.

## Deeper layer / true nature
Lysa is an informant for the [[harbor-cell]]. Her brother was conscripted and died under the
occupation; she has fed the cell the patrol rotation and manifest data for two years. She is not
an idealist — she is a griever with access — and that makes her cautious rather than zealous. She
will not take a risk she can't survive, and she has a private limit she has never told the cell:
she will not get civilians killed.

## Agenda / what they want
To see the garrison gone without the harbor — and the people who depend on it — burned down in the
process. Short term: keep her position above suspicion, because the moment she's compromised the
cell loses its eyes on the water.

## What they know
- The full harbor patrol rotation, including the gap on the night tide.
- That the [[harbor-cell]] is planning something larger than its usual pilfering.
- That the garrison quartermaster has started cross-checking her manifests — she is being watched,
  though she doesn't yet know how closely.

## Disposition toward the PC
Wary-transactional. She has no reason to trust strangers and every reason not to. If the PC can
show they're no friend of the garrison, she warms to guarded usefulness — information for
information. Push too fast and she goes opaque.

## Secrets
- [hidden] Lysa feeds the harbor patrol's rotation to the [[harbor-cell]].
  Known to: [[lysa-fenn]], harbor-cell leadership.
- [hidden] She will refuse, at the last moment, any plan she believes will get civilians killed —
  even if it means breaking with the cell.
  Known to: [[lysa-fenn]] only.
